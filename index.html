Redesign an e-commence website


Project Overview

A one person project from my User Experience Design Immerses program at General Assembly. Pretty Parlor is a Seattle local vintage boutique carries women and men vintage style clothing, also dresses for bride. However, customers can’t purchase clothes from Pretty Parlor’s website. The purchasing process has to go through Esty.

Project Plan

According to the interview with the store owner, I decided to incorporate the purchasing process and product listing service into Pretty Parlor's current website. Also, improve the website’s navigation system.

  • Understanding persona
  • Competitive study
  • Wireframing, hand sketching
  • Prototyping & Testing

Understanding Persona

For this particular project, the persona was given by the instructor team. Understanding the persona and break down the pain points are my primary focus in this process.

    Three areas I focused on during my design process

  • Navigation
  • New arrivals
  • Product infomarion and availability
